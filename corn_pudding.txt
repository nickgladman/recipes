Corn Pudding

# Ingredients

1 can, drained, whole sweet corn kernels
1 can cream corn
1 stick butter
8 oz of sour cream (do not use light or reduced fat sour cream)
1 box Jiffy Corn Muffin mix


# Methods

1. Melt butter.

2. Combine all ingredients together until homogenous and pour into cooking dish (aluminum tray or metal tray).

	* Note: you can store the batter, covered, overnight and then bake it the next day

3. Cook at 400 degrees for 45-60 minutes, or until the surface turns a color of brown/golden brown to your liking.

4. Let sit for a bit and then serve.
